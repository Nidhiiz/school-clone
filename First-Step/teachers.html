Meet Founders (Husband Wife Duo)

Reshma and Ramakrishna have been at the forefront of early childhood education and development for over 20 years. Their commitment and dedication to providing excellence in education have resulted in First Steps becoming a leading standalone preschool brand in Kachiguda, Hyderabad. They have helped shape the minds and futures of countless children, and their impact extends beyond the classroom to after-school activities and preschool teacher training.

Mrs. Reshma Shetty

Founder and Principal of First Steps Preschool

Reshma Shetty, the Founder and Principal of First Steps Preschool, with over two decades of experience, her commitment and dedication to providing excellence in education have resulted in First Steps becoming a leading standalone preschool brand in Kachiguda, Hyderabad.

She has helped shape the minds and futures of countless children, and is the driving force behind the excellence in education that the school is known for. With a commerce background, Reshma did a Certificate Course in early childhood and care and has attended many workshops to continuously develop her skills.

She completed the 10,000 Women Entrepreneurship Program sponsored by Goldman Sachs at ISB and recently did the Advanced Woman Ambassador Program at ISB as well. Reshma is passionate about empowering women entrepreneurs and aims to provide financial independence to women to facilitate a better life for them.

Ramakrishna Shetty

Co-Founder of First Steps Preschool

Ramakrishna Shetty is a seasoned entrepreneur with over two decades of experience in various industries, including hospitality and education. As the founder director of First Steps Preschool in Kachiguda, Hyderabad, he has played a key role in shaping the preschool’s vision and strategy.

With a degree in Commerce, a post Graduation in Business Management, and a post Diploma in Hotel Management, Ramakrishna brings a unique blend of business acumen and industry expertise to his role. He has a deep understanding of the challenges involved in running a successful business, as well as a passion for education that motivates him to provide the best possible learning experience for children.

Under his leadership, First Steps Preschool has become a leading name in early childhood education in Hyderabad, with a strong focus on building a strong foundation for the future generation.

We are proudly the best Pre-School situated at Kachiguda, Hyderabad!
