              FAQ's

              How does Angel Kids differ from other pre-schools in Kachiguda, Hyderabad?
              Angel Kids is not just any pre-school, it's a magical wonderland for your little ones! Our integrated curriculum, inspired by the Montessori philosophy, offers hands-on learning experiences that will tickle your child's curiosity and imagination. We understand that the first five years of a child's life are full of wonder and adventure, and our nurturing and stimulating environment will make sure your little angel enjoys every moment of it! Our comprehensive early childhood education programme is tailored to meet the needs and expectations of busy parents who want the best for their precious cherubs. Come and join the Angel Kids family, where learning is fun and every day is an adventure!

              Angel Kids is a unit of Ratik Edufun Pvt. Ltd. which was incorporated on 21st January, 2000. It has successfully completed 21 glorious years and faced many challenges in its journey. With each challenge it has only grown stronger. One of the pioneers in starting Play Group in Hyderabad, it started with 5 kids and by the end of the year reached 10 kids and over the last two decades has more than 3,000 satisfied parents.
              What age group of children do you enroll?

              We enroll children aged 2-6 years in our pre-school programs at Angel Kids in Kachiguda, Hyderabad.

              What is your curriculum and approach to teaching?

              Our curriculum is play-based and designed to promote social, emotional, cognitive, and language skills in young children. We prioritize personalized attention and individualized learning plans for each child.

              What are the qualifications of your teachers?

              Our teachers are experienced and qualified professionals with a passion for early childhood education. They hold degrees in Early Childhood Education and undergo regular training to stay up-to-date with the latest teaching techniques.

              What extra-curricular activities do you offer?

              We offer a range of creative arts, music, dance, and physical fitness programs to promote well-rounded development in our students.

              How do you ensure the safety and security of the children?

              We prioritize the safety and security of our students and have strict protocols in place, including CCTV surveillance, regular cleaning and disinfection, and background checks for all staff members.

              How do you communicate with parents?

              We have an open-door policy and communicate regularly with parents through daily reports, progress updates, parent-teacher meetings, and newsletters.

              How can I enroll my child in Angel Kids?

              How does Angel Kids' communication with parents compare to other pre-schools?

              We prioritize open and regular communication with parents through daily reports, progress updates, parent-teacher meetings, and newsletters, to keep parents involved and informed in their child's education and development.
